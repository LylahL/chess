Pawn:

Moves forward one square, but captures diagonally.
On its first move, a pawn can advance two squares.
Rook:

Moves horizontally or vertically as many squares as you want.
Knight:

Moves in an "L" shape: two squares in one direction and one square perpendicular.
The only piece that can "jump" over others.
Bishop:

Moves diagonally as many squares as you want.
Queen:

Combines the moves of the rook and bishop.
Moves horizontally, vertically, or diagonally as many squares as you want.
King:

Moves one square in any direction.
Cannot move into check (threatened by opponent's pieces).
